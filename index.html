The 2017 Eclipse

The NEW Erehwon Lodge at Western Wings invites you to our Total
Solar Eclipse Party. Eastern Idaho has been listed as one of the Greatest
Places to view the Total Solar Eclipse on August 21, 2017. This is sure
to be one of natures most Spectacular Sights!

We are located right in the heart of the path of totality. We have an incredible viewing time of over
2 minutes and 16 seconds. The complete experience will last more than
2 and a half hours.
